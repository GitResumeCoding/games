Refresh & update Problems?

It seems like the browsers are holding onto the old versions tightly.

To release them refresh by using CTL+F5 at the same time for force a new pull from the server.

If that doesn't work wait for the physics engine to load (construct gear screen) then trigger the game to start and only then trigger a refresh with CTL+F5.

Level One - Avoid the Spikes (Portrait Mode)

Two - Collect the Circles (Landscape Mode)

Move your player around and collect the appearing circles before they disappear. You can also tap a location on the screen to warp jump to that location.

Three - Protect From Invader Swarms(Landscape Mode)

Three - Protect From Invader Swarms(Landscape Mode)

Move your player around in different directions to protect your location as the swarm invade your space.

Four - AsterBlaster (Portrait Mode)

Four - AsterBlaster (Portrait Mode)

Gobble up the Purple Asteroids Avoid the Blue Ectoplasm

Slide your ship sideways to catch them

If you are on a computer you can click the enemies as they appear to make the player rotate towards the enemy and blast them away. Or you can click and hold then the player will rotate and continuously blast away following your cursor.

If you are on a phone you can tap the enemies with your finger as they appear to make the player rotate towards the enemy and blast them away. Or you can tap and hold and then the player will rotate with you and continuously blast away following your finger.

Five - SnagMeSomeColors (Portrait Mode)

Five - SnagMeSomeColors (Portrait Mode)

Shoot the Bombs & avoid the Bombs from hitting you.

You'll lose health & points if you let the bombs drop without shooting them.

Collect the coins by shooting them and gain points. Every 100 Points you gain perfect health again!

Shoot the mushrooms and gain health as well as points.

Let the mushrooms hit you (zip sideways at the last second) and you gain even more health!

Don't let the bombs touch you or you die immediately and it's game over.
