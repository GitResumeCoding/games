Thanks for playing and testing individual level's code for my game. 
If you are playing after a code refresh and it seems like the old functionality is still there, allow the game to load in your browser completely then hit refresh on your browser to reload the game it should pull the new code from the server.
If you are certain it is not pulling the code, open your browser in incognito/privacy mode which forces the code be pulled completely fresh, as if you never ever saw my game code before.

Level One - Avoid the Spikes (Portrait Mode)

Move from side to side and avoid the falling spikes.

If you are on a computer, click on player and hold left mouse to drag player from side to side.

If you are on a phone simply slide finger on player.

Level One LINK

Two - Collect the Circles (Landscape Mode)

Move your player around and collect the appearing circles before they disappear.

If you are on a computer, left click player and move around the board, you can also simply click the white appearing circles to collect them.

If you are on a phone click player with finger and zip it around the board avoiding the enemies.

Level Two LINK

Three - Protect From Invader Swarms(Landscape Mode)

Fast pace swarms converge on your position, tap each enemy and blast them away before they get you.

If you are on a computer you can click the enemies as they appear to make the player rotate towards the enemy and blast them away. Or you can click and hold then the player will rotate and continuously blast away following your cursor.

If you are on a phone you can tap the enemies with your finger as they appear to make the player rotate towards the enemy and blast them away. Or you can tap and hold and then the player will rotate with you and continuously blast away following your finger.
